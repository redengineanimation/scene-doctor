## What it does

Downloaded and imported assets are full of silent problems: unapplied
transforms, flipped normals, textures tagged the wrong color space,
missing files, orphaned data, junk geometry. Most don't error — they
quietly wreck your shading, exports, or renders later.

Scene Doctor surfaces all of them in one panel:

1. **Run Diagnosis** — one click scans the whole scene.
2. **Health score** tells you how bad it is, 0–100.
3. **Fix** each issue with one click — safely, with confirmation on
   anything destructive.

## Requirements

- Blender **4.2 or newer** (tested through 5.1).
- Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Scene Doctor comes in a free **Lite** build and a paid **Pro** build.
